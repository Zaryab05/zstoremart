Who we are

Z Mart is the leading online marketplace in South Asia, empowering tens of thousands of sellers to connect with millions of customers. Z Mart provides immediate and easy access to 100 producs in more than 2+ categories and delivers more than 50 packages every month to all corners of Karachi.

Z Mart is a mall, a marketplace and a community for its customers. It is also a university for entrepreneurs, and every month it educates more than 5,000 new sellers on e-commerce operations.

To overcome the logistics challenge in its markets, Z Mart has built its own logistics company specifically designed for e-commerce operation- to raise the standards for the industry. Z Mart is also helping existing and new logistics providers digitize.
